The C2B2 Compare Tool provides immediate, verifiable insight into the spatial relationships between any two marine data layers. By calculating Pearson's r and Spearman's œÅ across overlapping pixels, the tool allows users to quickly verify theoretical ecological impacts (like the effect of trawling on reefs) and identify critical data anomalies, such as spatial co-location bias where human pressures concentrate in high-value ecological areas. The output delivers clear metrics and an instant scatter plot for immediate visual diagnosis.



Predict Ecological Impact

Our C2B2 Predict Tool delivers robust, next-generation spatial forecasting. It automatically leverages superior Non-Linear Ensemble Models (Random Forest, GBM, XGBoost, and Neural Networks) and employs a critical log(x+1) transformation to reliably analyze zero-inflated marine density data. This approach effectively untangles complex drivers (ecology vs. human pressure) to provide planners with high-accuracy prediction maps, complete with crucial uncertainty and residual maps, giving confidence to optimize conservation zoning and resource management decisions.
